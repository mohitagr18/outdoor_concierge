Here is a thorough list of all data points being captured from our various API integrations:

üèõÔ∏è National Park Service (NPS) API
Captured via NPSClient & mapped in nps_adapter.py

1. Park Information (ParkContext)
Identity: parkCode, fullName, description, url (Official Page).
Location: Latitude, Longitude (float).
Contacts: Phone numbers (ext/desc), Email addresses.
Operating Hours: Detailed schedules (Mon-Sun), exceptions, and seasonal notes.
Images: High-res URL, Title, Alt Text, Caption, Credit.

2. Safety & Management (Alert)
id, title, description.
Category: Safety, Weather, Danger, Information, Closure.
url (Reference link), lastIndexedDate.

3. Lodging & Camping (Campground)
Details: id, name, description, reservationUrl, isOpen.
Capacity: Total sites, Tent-only sites, RV-only sites.
Accessibility: Detailed accessibility text (toilets, fire rings, wheelchair access).
Financials: Fees (Amount, Title, Description).
Contacts & Images: Specific phone/email and photos for each campground.

4. Facilities & Operations (VisitorCenter)
id, name, description, url.
Location: Lat/Lon and Physical/Mailing Addresses (Line 1-3, City, State, ZIP).
Operations: Independent operating hours and contact info.

5. Real-Time Viewers (Webcam)
id, title, description.
Stream Info: url (Page), imageUrl (Direct stream source), isStreaming (bool).
Management: status (Active/Inactive), relatedParks.

6. Exploration & Education (Place, ThingToDo, PassportStamp)
Places: Title, Listing description, Body text, isOpenToPublic, isManagedByNps, Tags.
Activities: Title, Short/Long description, duration (e.g., "3-4 hours"), season (Spring/Summer), activities (Hiking, Biking).
Rules: arePetsPermitted, isReservationRequired.
Philately: PassportStamp Label, Type (Physical/Virtual), location mapping
